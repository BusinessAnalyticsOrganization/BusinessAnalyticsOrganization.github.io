The Business Analytics Organization (BAO) is a student organization that fosters interaction and networking with industry experts and leaders in the field of analytics and data science. Workshops and networking events expose students to different technologies and application areas.

​

Becoming a BAO Member

To become a BAO member the steps are as follows-

  • $25 Fee to be assessed by quick-paying (Zelle) our Treasurer, Sravya Tangirala. (see contact information below)

Once we receive your payment, Sravya will reach out with an email and link to our membership information survey.

This is all that goes into obtaining a lifetime membership with BAO and following your joining & filling out of our survey we will include you in all of our organization-wide communications and Slack channel. Our communications include our bi-weekly email, which covers a plethora of topics like upcoming events and interesting articles about applications of analytics or concepts. If you are still interested in joining, please reference our Treasurer’s QuickPay/Zelle information below and follow the above steps. If you have any questions or doubts, please feel free to contact us via our organization email!
