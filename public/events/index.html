Events

The Business Analytics Organization (BAO) is a student organization that fosters interaction and networking with industry experts and leaders in the field of analytics and data science. Workshops and networking events expose students to different technologies and application areas.

​

Events hosted by BAO include:

  • Coffee Chats and Brown Bags, where professors speak about their research and share an informal conversation with students.

  • Speaker Series, where leaders in the industry come and speak to our members about their career’s trajectory and what they do in their role.

  • Alumni Panel Events, where former students come and speak to our members about their experience in the workforce.

  • Hack-A-Thons and Analytics Challenges- great opportunities to work on real problems and bolster your resume.

  • Professional Development Workshops- building your resume and working on the skills required to launch your career.

  • Social Events- dinners and other opportunities to meet and interact with other members.

​

We typically try to offer a mix of events each semester based on interest amongst our members. All events typically have a limited number of seats and require an RSVP which will be included in communications about the specific event! We offer these spots on a first come, first serve basis but hope to offer enough events for all members to participate across a given semester.

If you have any questions or doubts about an event feel free to contact a E-Board member in our Slack group or directly via email, we are happy to shed some light on any questions you may have!
